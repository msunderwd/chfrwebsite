4.3.1 Russel I/C to Nolan Yard


Train starts at Russell I/C staging (MP 0), proceeds Southbound (counter-clockwise) on blue main track to Nolan Yard (MP 60).
Optionally, train continues on red and blue main tracks for multiple loops to extend run. 
Train ends at Nolan Yard A/D Track (MP 60).

Dispatcher's note:  Train can be held at MP 72 or at points end of North crossover at Glover's Bend (MP 83).

4.3.5 Mine Branch


From Nolan Yard (MP 60) train proceeds Southbound along Nolan Yard - Twin Bridge Hill route.  Pull past switch at MP 65, and back to the mine.
Train may optionally use crossover in red to access mine or perform run-around.  Red crossover is currently not operational.

4.3.6 Town Branch and Loscher Marbles


From Nolan Yard, train proceeds along NY-TH-GB route. 

For access to Loscher Marbles, train cuts off, leaving cars at GB Station if needed, pulls through switchback shown in red.  For access to Town Branch industries, train pulls past switch at MP 5 and backs into spur shown in blue.

Note.  Trains must have authorization from the Dispatcher to occupy the main for Town Branch switching operations.  Unless specifically authroized, the MP 85 switch must be lined for the main.
